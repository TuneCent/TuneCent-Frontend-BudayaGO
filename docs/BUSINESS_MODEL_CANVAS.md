## 1. KEY PARTNERS (Mitra Kunci)

### Pemerintah & Regulator

#### **Kemendikbudristek** (Kementerian Pendidikan, Kebudayaan, Riset, dan Teknologi)

**Value for Them**:
- Platform digital untuk monitoring & support ekonomi kreatif musik
- Data real-time: berapa musisi aktif, funding terkumpul, regions covered
- Tool untuk achieve target pemerintah: digitalisasi budaya, pemberdayaan UMKM kreatif

**Value for Us**:
- **Endorsement resmi** pemerintah (credibility boost)
- Akses ke database musisi tradisional terdaftar
- **Co-funding grants**: Kemendikbud bisa match 50% dari fan investment (contoh: fans invest Rp 10 juta, Kemendikbud add Rp 5 juta)
- **Verified badge**: Musisi yang di-verify Kemendikbud dapat badge khusus (trust signal)

**Partnership Model**:
- MoU resmi (Memorandum of Understanding)
- Dashboard analytics untuk Kemendikbud (white-label view)
- Co-branded campaigns: "Program Digitalisasi Musik Nusantara powered by TuneCent x Kemendikbudristek"
- Quarterly report: Impact metrics (musisi empowered, funding generated, cultural heritage preserved)

**KPI Partnership**:
- 10 provinsi get co-funding program (Year 1)
- Rp 500 juta government matching fund (Year 1)
- 100 musisi verified by Kemendikbud

#### **Dinas Kebudayaan Provinsi/Kota** (34 provinsi, 500+ kota/kabupaten)

**Value for Them**:
- Digital tools untuk onboarding & monitoring musisi daerah
- Reporting system ke pusat (Kemendikbud) - automated, no manual data entry
- Success metrics untuk APBD budget allocation (show impact of cultural budget spending)

**Value for Us**:
- **Access to local musician database** (each Dinas punya list musisi tradisional di wilayahnya)
- **Government stamp of approval** (logo Dinas di platform = trust)
- **Local partnership activation**: Dinas refer musisi â†’ TuneCent training & onboarding
- **Budget allocation**: Dinas bisa invest ke campaign musisi lokal pakai dana APBD (guaranteed funding untuk pilot campaigns)

**Partnership Model**:
- **White-label dashboard**: Dinas bisa login dan lihat:
  - Jumlah musisi di wilayah mereka yang aktif di TuneCent
  - Total funding yang masuk ke musisi lokal
  - Impact metrics: income increase, campaign success rate
- **Artist onboarding program**:
  - Dinas host workshop bersama TuneCent (offline + online)
  - Training: "Cara Crowdfund Musik dengan Blockchain"
  - TuneCent provide facilitator + materials
- **Co-investment scheme**:
  - Dinas allocate Rp 100 juta dari APBD
  - Each campaign musisi lokal get Rp 5-10 juta co-funding
  - Prioritas: musisi dengan heritage cultural value tinggi (gamelan, musik daerah hampir punah)

**Scaling Plan**:
- **Year 1**: 10 Dinas pilot (Jateng, Jabar, DIY, Jatim, Bali, Sumut, Sumbar, Sulsel, Kalbar, NTT)
- **Year 2**: 20 Dinas additional (expand ke seluruh Jawa, Sumatera, Sulawesi)
- **Year 3**: 34 Dinas (all provinces)

**Pricing (White-label SaaS)**:
- Rp 10 juta/bulan per provinsi untuk dashboard access & support
- Target Year 1: 10 provinsi = Rp 100 juta/bulan = Rp 1.2 miliar/tahun revenue

**Value Exchange**:

  - Funding platform (easy crowdfunding)

  - Revenue share (komunitas dapat komisi 5% dari campaign member mereka yang sukses)
- **Komunitas provide**:
  - Musician onboarding (refer members untuk join TuneCent)
  - Endorsement (komunitas leader pakai TuneCent = social proof)

**Activation Program**:

  - Mahasiswa yang lulus ISI bisa langsung launch campaign di TuneCent (fast-track onboarding)

**Why This Works**:
- ISI students = future professional musicians (early adoption)
- Campus evangelism = word-of-mouth marketing yang powerful
- Alumni network = long-term community building

### Musician Onboarding & Curation

**Onboarding Flow**:
1. **Discovery**: Artist dengar tentang TuneCent (via community, campus, social media)
2. **Registration**: Sign up (email + KYC for high-value campaigns)
3. **Profile Setup**: Upload bio, photo, sample music, social media links
4. **Verification**: Team review + optional Dinas Kebudayaan verification (verified badge)
5. **Training**: Video tutorial + 1-on-1 onboarding call (untuk pilot artists)
6. **Campaign Creation**: Artist create first campaign dengan guidance
7. **Launch**: Campaign go live, artist promote ke fans

**Curation Strategy**:
- **Quality Control**: First 100 uploads manually reviewed (prevent spam/plagiarism)
- **Prioritization**: Gamelan, angklung, music daerah langka get priority featuring (homepage hero section)
- **Support Tiers**:
  - **White Glove** (Top 10% artists): Dedicated account manager, marketing support, priority customer service
  - **Standard** (Rest): Self-service dengan email/chat support

**KPI**:
- 50 musicians onboarded (Q3 2025)
- 500 musicians (Q4 2025)
- 1,000+ musicians (Q1 2026)
- Campaign launch rate: 70% (70% of onboarded musicians launch min 1 campaign within 3 months)

### Crowdfunding Operations

**Campaign Management**:
- **Pre-Launch Review**: Team review campaign sebelum go live (fraud prevention)
- **Launch Support**: Help artist promote (featured di homepage, social media shoutout)
- **Progress Monitoring**: Track campaign progress, intervene jika stuck (suggest strategy)
- **Post-Campaign**:
  - Successful: Fund release (milestone-based), prompt artist untuk deliver
  - Unsuccessful: Refund investors atau extend deadline (with artist approval)

**Fund Management**:
- **Escrow Smart Contract**: Dana di-hold di smart contract sampai milestone achieved
- **Milestone-Based Release**:
  - 50% released saat campaign end (untuk start production)
  - 50% released saat artist upload final deliverable (album/song)
- **Investor Protection**: Jika artist tidak deliver dalam 6 bulan, investors bisa vote untuk refund

**Transparency Dashboard**:
- Public dashboard showing:
  - Total funding raised (all-time)
  - Campaign success rate
  - Top campaigns by funding
  - Top investors by total investment

3. **Government Partnership**: MoU dengan Kemendikbud & Dinas = barrier to entry competitor
4. **Brand**: Strong cultural positioning ("TuneCent = platform untuk musik Nusantara")
5. **Technology**: Smart contract IP & know-how (audited, battle-tested)

---

### Risks to Monitor

1. **Regulatory**: Blockchain/crypto regulation di Indonesia masih grey area
   - Mitigation: Dual payment (crypto + fiat), proactive engagement dengan regulator
2. **Adoption**: Non-crypto native users might find platform intimidating
   - Mitigation: Embedded wallet (Privy), simplified UX, education program
3. **Music Copyright Dispute**: Potential plagiarism atau claim ownership issues
   - Mitigation: Audio fingerprinting, manual review, dispute resolution process
4. **Competition**: Spotify atau platform besar bisa copycat fitur
   - Mitigation: Focus on niche (traditional music), build community moat, speed of execution
